Kingston Fun Day returns to the recreation ground for a day of live music, local food, and proper South Devon good vibes. Free entry — just bring yourself, your mates, and your dancing shoes.

  • Saturday 1 August 2026 — main event day
  • 10 bands on the poster, one marquee, maximum energy
  • Camping Fri 2 Aug – Sun 4 Aug (book below)
  • Location Kingston Recreation Ground, Knightsbridge
